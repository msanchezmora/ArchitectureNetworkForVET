#EuropeanArchitectureNetwork

A pan European architecture network able to work together on Vocational Educational Training

We are a Leonardo Association grouping of architecture centres, Faculties of Architecture and vocational training organisations. Our aim is to develop closer links between vocational education and training (VET) and the world of work in the areas of urbanism/town planning, architecture, landscape architecture and other built environment professions. Members of this group are: MADE (UK) , Colaborativa (Spain), Wroclaw University of Technology (Poland), University of Brussels (Belgium) and Vuste Envis (Czech Republic).

This interactive map puts together relevant formal and informal training initiatives in Europe. The objective is to facilitate connections and spread out their works. If you would like to be part of the network please send us an email.
